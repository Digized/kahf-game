---

## How to Play

### Controls
- **Mouse/Touch:** Drag to rotate camera
- **Scroll/Pinch:** Zoom in/out
- **Choices:** Click text options to advance

### Patience Mechanic
- You begin with **3 patience**
- Questioning Khidr costs patience
- Stay silent to preserve patience
- Different endings based on your choices

### Features
- **Real-time 3D environments** - Boat journeys, villages, towns
- **Animated events** - Boat breaking, wall repair, revelation visions
- **Atmospheric visuals** - Warm desert palette, low-poly aesthetic
- **Branching narrative** - Your choices shape the story

## Technical Details

**Built with:**
- Three.js r147 (3D rendering)
- Vanilla JavaScript (no frameworks)
- Google Fonts (Cinzel, Crimson Text)
- Pure CSS animations

**Optimized for:**
- Desktop browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Mobile/tablet (touch-friendly controls)
- Lightweight (~50KB total, excluding Three.js CDN)

Designed for **24-30 year old gamers** who appreciate:

- Meaningful choices with real stakes
- Atmospheric, intentional aesthetics

## Themes

- **Patience vs Knowledge** - Sometimes understanding comes through waiting, not demanding
- **Divine Wisdom** - Actions that seem cruel may hide profound mercy
- **Humility** - Even great wisdom has limits
- **Trust** - Faith in what cannot be immediately seen or understood
