# Moses & Khidr - Interactive Fiction Prototype

## About

A web-based interactive fiction experience exploring the story of Moses and Khidr from Surah Al-Kahf (Quran 18:60-82).

**Core Theme:** Patience in the face of incomprehensible divine wisdom.

**Gameplay:** Choice-driven narrative with a patience mechanic. The player must decide when to question and when to trust.

## Features

- **Patience System:** Three questions allowed (matching the story structure)
- **Meaningful Choices:** Each decision reflects Moses's struggle between curiosity and trust
- **Multiple Endings:** Based on how many times you question Khidr
- **Emotional Impact:** Designed to make the player *feel* Moses's frustration and ultimate humility

## How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser
2. Read the narrative text
3. Choose your responses carefully
4. Watch the patience meter
5. Experience the revelation

## Technical Details

**Stack:**
- Pure HTML/CSS/JavaScript (no dependencies)
- Runs entirely in browser
- Works offline
- Mobile-friendly responsive design

**Files:**
- `index.html` - Structure

- `game-data.js` - All story content and scenes

- `README.md` - This file

## Development Notes

**Design Decisions:**
- Kept UI minimal to focus on narrative
- Used patience bar as visual feedback
- Highlighted "question" choices in red to emphasize weight
- Fade-in animations for smooth reading experience
- Two distinct endings based on patience level

**Lessons Embedded:**
1. Divine wisdom transcends human understanding
2. Patience is harder than knowledge
3. Apparent cruelty may be hidden mercy
4. Humility is essential, even for the wise
